# Brinker International

Brinker International is a leading casual dining restaurant company that owns, operates, and franchises Chili's Grill & Bar and Maggiano's Little Italy. Headquartered in Dallas, Texas, the company operates over 1,600 Chili's locations across 29 countries and over 50 Maggiano's locations in the United States.

## Brands

- **Chili's Grill & Bar** - Casual dining chain with 1,600+ locations globally, known for Tex-Mex cuisine, burgers, and fajitas
- **Maggiano's Little Italy** - Upscale casual Italian-American dining with 50+ US locations

## Digital Presence

Brinker operates digital ordering, loyalty programs, and mobile applications for both brands. The company uses Prismic CMS for digital content management across production and QA environments.

## Operations

- Operates company-owned and franchised restaurants
- 29 countries for Chili's international presence
- Digital ordering and loyalty programs
- Point-of-sale integrations for restaurant operations
